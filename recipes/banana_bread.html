Bean's Banana Bread

Ingredients

Bread

  • 12" pan (prefereably seasoned cast iron)
  • 8oz Butter
  • 1.5 Cups Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • 4 Ripe Bananas
  • 1.5 Cups Flour
  • 1.5 t Baking Soda
  • 1 t salt
  • Butter for pan
  • Pecans
  • Zest of 2 lemons

Topping

  • 3 oz Butter
  • (Optional) Sliced banana

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F
  2. In a large mixing bowl cream the 8oz butter and sugar with a mixer until pale and fluffy
  3. Mix in the eggs until fully incorporated
  4. Mix in the smashed bananas and lemon zest
  5. Mix in the flour, baking soda and salt until all items are fully incorporated
  6. Rub unmelted butter on pan until thinly covered
  7. Pour the batter into the cast iron pan and place in oven
  8. (optional) After 10 minutes remove bread from oven and place slice bananas on top. Place back in oven
  9. Total cooking time = 45minutes or when middle is no longer liquid. If you oven runs hot you may need to reduce temperature. Pay attention
  10. Remove from oven and brush with melted butter
